### 1. ✅ **Note Persistence** - COMPLETE

**Location**: `index.html:200-217, renderer.js:47-62`
- **Implementation**: 16 predefined gradient themes with CSS classes
- **Features**: Live theme switching, persistence across sessions
- **Quality**: Beautiful modern gradients with accessibility considerations

### 4. ✅ **Keyboard Shortcuts** - COMPLETE
**Location**: `main.js:163-175, renderer.js:117-144`
- **Implementation**: Global and local keyboard shortcuts
- **Shortcuts Available**:
  - `Cmd/Ctrl+N`: New note
  - `Cmd/Ctrl+S`: Manual save
  - `Cmd/Ctrl+W`: Close note
  - `Cmd/Ctrl+Q`: Quit app
  - `Escape`: Close color picker
- **Quality**: Standard platform conventions followed

  - Proper focus management
- **Quality**: Excellent macOS integration with vibrancy effects

### 7. ✅ **Auto-Save Functionality** - COMPLETE
**Location**: `renderer.js:31-38, 95-98`
- **Implementation**: Multi-trigger auto-save system

- **Quality**: Comprehensive and reliable
